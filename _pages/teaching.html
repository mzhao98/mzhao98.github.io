My teaching is informed by training from the CMU Eberly Center for Teaching Excellence and Educational Innovation, where I developed skills in inclusive classroom practices, effective lecture delivery, constructive feedback, and accessible course design.

During my time at Carnegie Mellon University, I was a co-instructor with Zackory Erickson for Introduction to Human-Robot Interaction: Foundations (Spring 2025), where I was involved in course delivery, curriculum development, and lectures. In addition, I was also a teaching assistant for undergraduate and graduate courses on Human-Robot Interaction (Fall 2022, Spring 2022). Prior to that, at Caltech, I served as a teaching assistant for courses spanning machine learning and introductory programming (2017–2020).
