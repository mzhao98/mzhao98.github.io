M.S. and Undergraduate Students

If you are interested in joining our lab as a graduate or undergraduate student, please reach out to me via email at mzhao2@andrew.cmu.edu. We are always looking for motivated students who are passionate about human-robot interaction and autonomous systems. We work on building human-centered robot learners and collaborators.
Please email me with your CV and a brief statement of research interests if you are interested in joining our lab.

Ph.D. Students

For Ph.D. applicants, please apply to the IU Bloomington Department of Computer Science Ph.D. program, and include my name in your application. Feel free to email me, as I am excited to further discuss your research interests!
